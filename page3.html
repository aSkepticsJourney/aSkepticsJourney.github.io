I'm open to sharing my journey with groups in a variety of settings and venues. My journey has proved valuable to both the skeptic as well as the Christian who may have spiritual questions and/or doubts.

To the right is a sample of my speaking and slide style.  Below you will find format options and content outlines that can be used at your event. Visit the Events page for past event information and About My Journey to learn more about me. In all formats, I weave my personal journey into presentations to relate with the audience.

I do not charge any speaker fee. If necessary, I may request compensation to cover travel, lodging, and/or meal expenses.

Please contact me to discuss opportunities at aSkepticsJourney@gmail.com

Formats

Journey Only

I present my Journey (testimony) from Atheism to Christianity.  I briefly introduce the lines of evidence that convinced me to change my mind.  

Perfect for Sunday church service(s).   

~30-45 minutes

Journey + Seminar

In addition to a seperate talk on my Journey, I'll also share evidence that convinced me to change my mind.

This format works best presenting my Journey in service(s) with the seminar following.

Journey: ~30 minutes
Seminar: ~2 hours 

Seminar Only

I'll share detailed evidence that convinced me to change my mind.  I weave my Journey into both presentations.

~3 hours with breaks

Custom

Multi-night events spanning 30-45 minute speaking sessions.

Or customize to your particular needs
