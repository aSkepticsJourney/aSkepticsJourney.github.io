I'm open to sharing my journey with groups in a variety of settings and venues. My journey has proved valuable to both the skeptic as well as the Christian who may have spiritual questions and/or doubts.

See this video for a sample of my speaking and slide style.  Below you will find format options and content outlines that can be used at your event. Visit the Events page for past event information and About My Journey to learn more about me. In all cases, I weave my personal journey into presentations to relate with the audience.

I do not charge any speaker fee.  I may request compensation to cover travel, lodging, and/or meal expenses.

Please contact me to discuss opportunities at aSkepticsJourney@gmail.com

Presentation Options

Journey Only

I present my Journey (testimony) from Atheism to Christianity.  I briefly introduce the lines of evidence that convinced me to change my mind.

Perfect for Sunday service(s).   

~30 minutes

Journey + Seminar

After an initial talk on my Journey, I share detailed evidence that convinced me to change my mind.

Works great for Sunday service(s) with a seminar following.

Seminar Only

~3 hours incl. breaks

Weekly / Weekend

Session Outlines

The Journey

A turning point in my life forces me to seek the evidence for God. Expecting to find “uneducated” Christians, I find the opposite. Experts on both side, looking at the same evidence, disagree. Eventually, my fear is overcome by a pesky thing called “truth”. I change my mind (twice) and head down a different path.
