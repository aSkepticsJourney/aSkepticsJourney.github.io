Presentation Content

Evidence For God

Design

There is a design in life and a design in our world.   Science, particularly in the last 100 years, has discovered evidence that is best explained by a creator rather than by nature.

Supernatural

Morality, near death experiences, emotions, our senses, and consciousness are evidence of things beyond nature and materials.

Evil and Suffering

Evil and Suffering is an obvious reality of our world.  This is often cited as evidence against the existence of a God.  I'll explain why I believe it's the opposite.

Evidence For Christianity

Change

The world changed in the most radical way starting 2000 years ago.  We'll explore this change and why I consider it radical.

Sources

The who, what, when, why of the radical change comes to us from three written sources:

1. Writers unfriendly to Christianity
2. The disciples who were taught by the eyewitnesses (a chain of custody)
3. The bible.  Now, I thought the bible was a made up story that grew over time.  I was wrong and I'll present evidence that the bible is in fact, more fact than fiction.

Eyewitness Testimony

When I came to realize the gospels were eyewitness accounts, it was a game changer in my journey.  We'll look at their testimony to determine if it is trustworthy using tests for any eyewitness.

Miracles

As a skeptic, miracles were not possible.  After looking at evidence, I concluded undeniably that at least one miracle has occurred.  And if one miracle is possible, just one, any miracle is possible including a resurrection. 
 
We'll explore if  the resurrection is the best explanation for the evidence compared to others.

Unique

What about all those other religions, hundreds of them.  Aren't they all basically the same and lead to the same place?  No, they all make exclusive claims.   Christianity is unique in many ways and if true, the others are false.
