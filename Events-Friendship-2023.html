Evidence For God

Design in Life

There is a blueprint in every human cell called the DNA molecule. Molecular machines use this blueprint to create life. Both are evidence of a design from a Creator.

Design in the World

Recent scientific discoveries support the big bang model and have exposed evidence of design in our solar system and the Universe.  

Supernatural

Morality, near death experiences, emotions, our senses, and consciousness are evidence of things beyond nature and materials.

Something is Wrong

Evil and suffering is an obvious reality of our world. This is often cited as evidence against the existence of a God. I'll explain why I believe it's the opposite.

Evidence for Christianity

Change

The world changed in the most radical way starting ~2000 years ago. We'll explore this change and why I consider it radical.

Sources

The who, what, where, when, and why of the radical change comes to us from multiple historical sources including the Bible.

I thought the Bible was a made up story that grew over time. I was wrong and I'll present evidence that the Bible is in fact, more fact than fiction.

Eyewitness
Testimony

When I came to realize the Gospels were eyewitness accounts, it was a game changer. We'll put their testimony to the test to determine truthfulness.

Miracles

As a skeptic, miracles were not possible. After looking at evidence, I concluded undeniably that at least one miracle has occurred. And if one miracle is possible, any miracle is possible.

We'll explore if the resurrection is the most reasonable explanation for the evidence.

Unique

What about all those other religions? Aren't they all basically the same and lead to the same place? No, they all make exclusive claims. Christianity is unique in many ways and if true, the others are false.
