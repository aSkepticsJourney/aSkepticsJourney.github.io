Speaking Fees

I’d be honored to speak at your event! While I don’t require a speaker fee, I deeply appreciate any compensation or travel reimbursement—though it’s entirely optional.
Any payment or donation goes directly to the non-profit Global Link Partners, helping fund mission trips and speaking engagements at small venues that otherwise couldn’t afford them.
It’s a win-win—more people get to hear the powerful evidence for God and Christianity, and together, we make an even greater impact! Let’s inspire, equip, and encourage people in their faith journey. Let’s make it happen!
