I enjoy sharing my journey with groups in a variety of settings and venues.  My journey has proven valuable to both skeptics as well as Christians.  The skeptic will hear evidence that may prompt them to take a harder look at God and Christianity.  The Christian will see evidence that supports their faith.

A Skeptic's Journey

A turning point in my life prompts me to seek the evidence for God. Expecting to find “uneducated” Christians, I find the opposite. Experts on both side, looking at the same evidence, yet disagree. Eventually, my fears are overcome by a pesky little thing called the “truth”. I change my mind (twice actually) and head down a different path.
